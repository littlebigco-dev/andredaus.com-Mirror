A focused 90-minute session designed to surface the assumptions embedded in a specific decision, strategy, or situation you're currently navigating.

## What It Is

Not a coaching session in the conventional sense. Not a consultancy brief. A structured adversarial examination of the reasoning behind one clearly defined problem.

You bring the problem as you understand it. I bring the questions you haven't been asked. We find out together what you've been treating as certain that deserves scrutiny.

## What You Leave With

- A clearer map of the assumptions your current thinking depends on
- At least one structural reframe of the problem you came with
- A set of questions to take back to your team or decision process
- Optionally: a short written summary of the key findings

## Who It's For

Leaders, founders, and senior decision-makers working through a specific strategic or operational challenge. This is not a discovery call — it's a working session. Come with a real problem.

## Format

90 minutes via video call. No preparation required beyond being willing to have your assumptions challenged.
