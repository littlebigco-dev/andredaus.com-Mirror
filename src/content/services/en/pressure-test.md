## The problem it solves

Most people who come to this session are not missing information. They have plenty of information. What they're missing is someone who will look at the reasoning underneath the decision and ask the question that stops them cold.

Your team will not ask that question. They're working for you, or with you, or toward the same outcome. Your advisors have a stake in your comfort. Your investors have a stake in the outcome.

I don't.

### What actually happens

You bring one clearly defined problem. It can be a decision you're navigating, a strategic situation that feels unresolved, or a doubt you can't shake.

I don't need preparation from you. I need you to be willing to have the reasoning behind your position examined — including the parts you've stopped examining yourself.

For ninety minutes, we apply structured adversarial scrutiny to the thinking behind your problem. Not hostile. Not contrarian for its own sake. Structured: finding the load-bearing beliefs your current thinking depends on, then pressure-testing each one.

You won't always like what surfaces. That's the point.

### Why ninety minutes

Long enough to get past the rehearsed version of the problem. Short enough that the investment is a decision, not a commitment. If one session changes how you see the problem — or confirms that your reasoning is solid — it has done its job.
