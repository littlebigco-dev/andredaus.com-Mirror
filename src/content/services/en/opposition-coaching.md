An ongoing coaching engagement for leaders who want to build the habit of adversarial thinking — not as a crisis tool, but as a permanent feature of how they reason.

## What It Is

Two sessions per month. Each session works with whatever is live in your world right now: a decision you're navigating, a pattern you keep noticing, a situation that isn't resolving the way it should.

This is not accountability coaching. It's not goal-setting. It's a sustained practice of having your reasoning examined by someone with no stake in your comfort.

## What Changes

Leaders who work through Opposition Coaching for 3–6 months typically report:
- A marked increase in their tolerance for genuine uncertainty
- Faster identification of the real question underneath presenting problems
- Reduced reliance on consensus as a proxy for correctness
- A changed relationship with dissent — seeking it rather than managing it

## Who It's For

Founders, C-suite, and senior leaders who already function at a high level but recognize that their biggest blind spots are the ones they can't see. This is not remedial — it's for people who want to get sharper.

## Format

2 sessions per month, 60 minutes each, via video call. Monthly commitment, 3-month minimum. Sessions are not recorded.
