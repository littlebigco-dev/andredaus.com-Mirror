A deep-format engagement for leadership teams working through a significant strategic decision, transition, or challenge. Half-day or full-day format depending on scope and complexity.

## What It Is

Most strategy offsites produce alignment. This produces something more valuable: a precise map of where your alignment is genuine and where it's social performance.

We work through your current strategic position using structured adversarial methodology — identifying the assumptions that are load-bearing, the scenarios you haven't modelled, and the dissent in the room that hasn't been heard.

## What You Leave With

- A documented assumption map for your current strategy
- Identified risk scenarios with probability weighting
- A concrete set of follow-up actions to test or revise key assumptions
- Team capability lift: your people leave knowing how to keep doing this

## Who It's For

Leadership teams of 3–10 people facing a significant decision: market entry, organizational restructure, product pivot, competitive response. The problem should be real and the stakes should matter.

## Format

Half-day (4 hours) or full-day (7 hours). Can be conducted in person at your offices or remotely via a dedicated virtual session environment. In-person strongly preferred for full-day format.
