question: "How does 1:1 coaching differ from consulting?"
answer: "Consulting addresses organizational problems. Coaching addresses how the individual thinks about problems. In practice, the two overlap — most strategic failures originate in the unchallenged assumptions of a single decision-maker. The 1:1 engagement focuses on your reasoning patterns, your blind spots, and the beliefs you've stopped noticing. The goal is that you make better decisions even when I'm not in the room."
