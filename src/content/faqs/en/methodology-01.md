question: "What exactly is Strategic Opposition — is this just red team thinking?"
answer: "Red Team thinking is the foundation — a military methodology for stress-testing plans through structured adversarial challenge. Strategic Opposition applies that framework to business, leadership, and organizational decision-making. The core discipline is the same: systematic, structured challenge of the assumptions your strategy depends on. What's different is how that methodology is transferred to you so you can keep doing it independently."
