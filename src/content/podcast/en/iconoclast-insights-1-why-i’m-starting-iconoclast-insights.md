Most advice out there is recycled nonsense. This podcast is different. I’ll be cutting through the noise, exposing blind spots, and sharing raw, unfiltered insights on business, psychology, and beyond.

If you’re tired of echo chambers and want a fresh perspective, stick around. In Episode 1, we’ll dive into a topic most people think they understand: Control.
