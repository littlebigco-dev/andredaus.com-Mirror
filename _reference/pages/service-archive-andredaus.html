    Assumption Hunting

    Find what your strategy depends on before the market does.

    A structured audit of the assumptions embedded in your strategy — not the ones you worry about, but the ones you've stopped noticing. Every strategic plan has load-bearing beliefs that have never been tested. I surface them, stress-test them, and give you a clear picture of where your thinking is resting on foundations that haven't been examined.

    1:1 Engagement · Individual

    1:1 Coaching

    A thinking partner without an agenda. Except honesty.

    For leaders who need rigorous intellectual opposition, not encouragement. I challenge your reasoning, expose the cognitive patterns you've built your decisions around, and help you see the assumptions you've mistaken for facts. Not therapy. Not cheerleading. Structured challenge to the way you think — so you make better decisions even when I'm not in the room.

  • ◻Burn the Box

    Blueprint or wireframe dissolving at edges. Dark, dramatic. NOT literal flames. 700×500px.

    Team Engagement · Project-based

    Burn the Box

    Not reframing. Systematic elimination of false limits.

    Complete deconstruction of the conceptual frameworks that define what your organisation believes is possible. Most teams have never questioned whether the boundaries they operate inside are real constraints or inherited assumptions. Burn the Box is the process of finding out — and deciding which boundaries deserve to survive scrutiny.

    Format
    Project-based
    Duration
    2–5 days
    Suitable for
    Leadership teams, departments
    View this service
  • ◈Workshop

    André leading a session — candid, mid-conversation. Reportage. NOT staged corporate. 700×500px.

    Workshop · Team

    Strategic Opposition Workshop

    Not a training on solutions. A confrontation with your own thinking.

    Your team learns to identify and dismantle the assumptions embedded in their decisions — not so they can use a new framework, but so they can keep doing it independently. The deliverable isn't a document. It's a capability your team retains when I'm gone.

    Group size

Not sure where to start?

Most engagements begin the same way

The discovery call isn't a sales call. It's thirty minutes of systematic questioning designed to surface the assumption most worth examining first.

Book a Discovery Call
  1. 1

    Discovery Call — no preparation needed

    30 minutes. I ask questions that surface the assumptions behind your most pressing problem. You leave with clarity regardless of whether we proceed.

  2. 2

    Scoped engagement — project or retainer

    Based on what the call surfaces, I'll recommend the most appropriate engagement. No ambiguity about what you're getting.

  3. 3

    Capability transfer — not dependency

    Every engagement ends with you in a stronger position to challenge your own thinking independently. A return engagement should be by choice — not because I've created a need for it.
