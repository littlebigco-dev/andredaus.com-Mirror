What happens in 30 minutes

1

I ask about the problem you've already defined

The decision you're stuck on, the situation that isn't improving, the strategy you're questioning. Whatever brought you here.

2

I question the problem definition itself

Most of the time, the framing of the problem contains the assumption that's actually causing it. That's what I'm looking for.

3

We determine whether to go further

If there's something worth pursuing, I'll be direct about what that looks like. If not, I'll tell you that too.
